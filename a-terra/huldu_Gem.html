Chapter 1: Defining Hidden Worlds

Conceptual Framework (Show/Hide)

Definitions of "hidden world" include spatial overlap, dimensional shifts, and symbolic realms.

  • Spatial Overlap: Worlds sharing physical space but imperceptible to ordinary senses.
  • Dimensional Shifts: Realms accessed through portals or changes in consciousness.
  • Symbolic Realms: Inner landscapes representing psychological or spiritual states.
